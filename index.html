About Me



My name is Maya Barnes. I am a software engineer with experience utilizing
JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby, Rails, MongoDB, Node.js, & Express.js.

I graduated from USC with a degree in business, before working in
investment management for 6 years and earning my CFA Charter.

On a personal note, I enjoy golfing, boxing, keeping up with the market,
and spending time with my corgi, Kevin.

Why Software Engineering?

The investment industry is plagued with painstakingly manual tasks and
archaic internal systems that hindered our productivity and client service.

While I found the dynamic problem solving and transformational client
experiences we delivered incredibly rewarding, I wanted to work on technologies
that improved lives, transformed industries, and generated a larger impact.

I believe software engineering is the forefront of innovation, and
am thrilled to be a part of that evolution.
